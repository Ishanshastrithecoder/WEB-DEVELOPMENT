About CHARUSAT

Charotar University of Science and Technology (CHARUSAT) is one of the fastest-growing private universities in Gujarat, India. With a vision to become a world-class institution, it provides high-quality education and research facilities across various disciplines.

Located in the serene town of Changa, CHARUSAT aims to nurture talent and shape young minds into future leaders, innovators, and professionals.

Courses Offered

CHARUSAT offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs through its constituent institutes:

  • B.Tech (Computer Engineering, IT, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, etc.)
  • M.Tech in specialized engineering fields
  • B.Pharm and M.Pharm (Pharmacy)
  • BBA, MBA (Management)
  • BCA, MCA (Computer Applications)
  • B.Sc. and M.Sc. (Science and Nursing)
  • Ph.D. programs in various disciplines
